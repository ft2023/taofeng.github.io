Tao Feng

I am a PhD student studying CS at UIUC, advised by Prof. Jiaxuan You. My research centers on building agentic AI systems that can autonomously reason, decide, remember, and collaborate. My mission is to push the frontier of LLM-powered agents through reinforcement learning, memory mechanisms, and graph-based intelligence. To achieve this, my work currently spans the following major research thrusts:

  • Agentic RL: Reinforcement learning for autonomous agent decision-making at scale.
  • Agentic Memory: Memory mechanisms enabling self-evolving and long-horizon agents.
  • Agentic LLM Reasoning: Teaching LLMs to reason and plan via RL-based training.
  • Agentic LLM Routing & Orchestration: Intelligent multi-LLM selection, routing, and collaboration.
  • Agentic Graph Intelligence: Graph-based frameworks for LLM agents and structured reasoning.
  • Agentic AI for Science: Deploying agentic systems to accelerate scientific discovery.

These thrusts are powered by rigorous research at the intersection of RL, Memory, LLMs, and Graph Learning, with the goal of architecting trustworthy AI agents that serve as partners for human advancement.
